About Engineering

Engineering is a broad discipine which involves the application of mathematics, empirical evidence and scientific, economic, social, and practical knowledge in order to invent, design, build, maintain, research, and improve, structures, machines, tools, systems, components, materials, and processes. It envelopes a wide range of sub-disciplines, each with a more specific emphasis on particular areas of applied science, technology and types of application.
